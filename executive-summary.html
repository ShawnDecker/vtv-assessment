Company Description

Value to Victory, LLC is an AI-powered personal development platform built on the proprietary P.I.N.K. Framework (People, Influence, Numbers, Knowledge, with Time as a 0.1x–2.0x multiplier). The platform launches with three consumer-facing verticals — life assessment, fitness coaching, and standards-based dating — that generate immediate revenue while the enterprise and licensing backend scales behind them.

VTV is the only platform combining multi-dimensional life assessment, AI-generated coaching, couples/dating compatibility, fitness integration (via FitCarna merger), and a Book-to-System conversion pipeline. Founded by U.S. Navy veteran and licensed real estate appraiser Shawn E. Decker. Infrastructure cost: $8–10K/mo. Already live and collecting users.
