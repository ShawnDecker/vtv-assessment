Testimony
From Darkness to Destiny

Born and raised in Moneta, VA, Bishop Norman was raised in church and taught to obey traditional values. After spending several years in the military, he found himself facing many troubling situations that led him into a life of drugs and immoral living. In March 1994, Pastor Norman was miraculously delivered from crack cocaine and was filled with God's love. This led him back to Christ and he began to saturate himself in the Word.

In 1999, Bishop Norman heard God's voice calling him to the pastoral ministry. With obedience to the call, the first service of Jesus Is Lord ~ Assembly of Love was held with seven people in attendance on October 10, 1999 at Fairview Elementary School. He furthered his education at Liberty University in Biblical studies, and on March 6, 2016, was consecrated to the office of Bishop.
