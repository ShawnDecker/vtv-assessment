About
A Journey of Destiny

Bishop Michael Penn, better known as "Pastor Penn," is married to Angela Kidd Penn and father of three — Jonathan, Sean, and Candice — with two grandchildren, Havyn and Jaxon. In 2000, Pastor Penn began his journey of destiny as Senior Pastor of what was then Galilee Missionary Baptist Church, now The Galilean House of Worship.
