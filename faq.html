Help & FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Assessment
What is the P.I.N.K. assessment?▼
A 25-question assessment measuring five pillars of your life: People, Influence, Numbers, Knowledge, and Time. Takes about 5 minutes. Your results show where you are strong and where you need to grow.
Is it free?▼
Yes. The assessment and your diagnostic report are always free. Premium reports, challenges, and coaching tools are available through membership.
Can I retake it?▼
Yes. Take it as many times as you want. Your progress page tracks every score so you can see how you grow over time.
Is this a clinical or diagnostic tool?▼
No. The Value Engine is a personal development and stewardship tool built on biblical principles. It is not a replacement for professional counseling, therapy, or medical advice.
Membership
What are the membership tiers?▼
VictoryPath ($29/mo) — individual tools, challenges, and action plans. Value Builder ($47/mo) — adds couple tools for both partners. Victory VIP ($497/mo) — includes coaching, VIP events, and all digital products.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. Cancel from your member dashboard or email valuetovictory@gmail.com. No contracts, no penalties.
How do I manage my subscription?▼
Log into your member dashboard and click "Manage Subscription." This opens the Stripe billing portal where you can update payment, change plans, or cancel.
Couples
How do couples use the platform?▼
On the Value Builder or Victory VIP plan, both partners create profiles and link accounts. You'll see shared reports, the relationship matrix, and can start the 30-Day Couple Challenge together.
Can I see my partner's individual scores?▼
Yes. Full transparency is the foundation of the couples program. Both partners consent to shared visibility upon enrollment. All scores, pillar maps, and growth tracking are fully visible to both.
