About VWC
A Word of Faith Church on the Move

Valley Word Church is an ethnically diverse, nondenominational Word of Faith Church of approximately 200 members on a beautiful 19-acre campus in Roanoke, Virginia. An active and growing body of believers reaching out to impact every nation for Christ.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the power of God's love, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and the guidance of the Holy Spirit. Through faith and prayer, we can overcome any obstacle and live a life of purpose and meaning.
