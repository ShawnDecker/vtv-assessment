About GHOW
A Church Centered on Christ

5078 A.L. Philpott Hwy

Martinsville, Virginia — A loving church family since 2000

The Galilean House of Worship is a nondenominational church in Martinsville, Virginia — a loving church family that enjoys worshipping and praising Jesus through traditional hymns, praise & worship, and contemporary music.

Bishop Michael Penn

Better known as "Pastor Penn," he is married to Angela Kidd Penn and father of three — Jonathan, Sean, and Candice. In the year 2000, Pastor Penn began his journey of destiny as Senior Pastor of what was then Galilee Missionary Baptist Church, now The Galilean House of Worship.
