Her Story
About
A Savannah local with a rare set of keys.

I’m Nikki Gergacs — a dedicated real estate professional passionate about helping people discover the charm and beauty of Savannah, Georgia. From historic squares draped in Spanish moss to coastal communities where the marsh meets the morning, Savannah has something for everyone, and I’ve spent my life learning her every block.

My family moved here when I was nine. After a Communications degree from the University of Louisiana at Monroe and undergrad core at Armstrong, I started my real estate career as an appraiser-trainee in Roanoke, Virginia in 2003. Two years later I came home, and I’ve been guiding Savannah buyers, sellers, and homeowners ever since — with 21+ years of appraisal experience now paired with active sales representation across Chatham, Bryan, and Effingham counties.

My ideal clients are luxury buyers, downsizers, upsizers, relocators, and lifestyle-driven professionals — people who want a guide more than a salesperson. The dual credential is the difference: most agents can show you a home; few can also tell you, with state-licensed precision, what it’s actually worth. I lead with relationship, not credentials. Let’s make your Savannah dream a reality.
