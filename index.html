About
Building Businesses. Serving Community.

Steadman Mclean is a Roanoke, Virginia entrepreneur and community leader. Originally from St. Catharines, educated at Virginia Western Community College (Class of 2010), Steadman builds businesses that serve families and strengthen communities. From childcare to bail bonds, every venture is rooted in meeting real needs for real people.

Married to Keriefa Mclean since April 14, 2007. Together they run Peek N Play Childcare and are building a legacy of service in the Roanoke Valley.
