About
Who I Am
From New Zealand to North Carolina

Brendon Albiston is a ministry leader, digital creator, Gallup Certified Strengths Coach, and entrepreneur. Born and raised in Whakatane, New Zealand, he graduated from Teen Mania Honor Academy (Class of 2001) and attended Domata School of Ministry before founding Albiston Family Ministries in 2005. Now based in Mount Airy, North Carolina, Brendon brings 21+ years of ministry experience across multiple ventures — all centered on helping people discover their identity as sons and daughters of God.
