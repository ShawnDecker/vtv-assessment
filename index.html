About
About VWC
A Word of Faith Church on the Move

Ethnically Diverse • ~200 Members

Situated on a beautiful 19-acre property along the I-81 corridor

Valley Word Church is an ethnically diverse, nondenominational Word of Faith Church. An active and growing body of believers reaching out to impact every nation for Christ through services, ministries, mission programs, books, DVDs, and their website.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the power of God's love, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and the guidance of the Holy Spirit. Through faith and prayer, we can overcome any obstacle and live a life of purpose and meaning.
