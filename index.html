About
About JLAL
A Church Born Through Prayer

From 7 Members to a Growing Ministry

Birthed through prayer in 1999 • Roanoke, VA

Jesus Is Lord ~ Assembly of Love is a non-denominational church centered on Jesus Christ and on the Holy Word of God. Under the leadership of Bishop Steven and Elder LaVerne Norman, this ministry continues to grow throughout Roanoke — sharing God's love and increasing lives through the Gospel.

Bishop Steven A. Norman

In 1999, Bishop Norman heard God's voice calling him to pastoral ministry. Consecrated to the office of Bishop on March 6, 2016. Under his leadership, JLAL has grown from 7 members to a thriving ministry impacting Roanoke and beyond.

Full Bio

Elder LaVerne Norman

Co-founder and Elder. Partner in ministry from the very first prayer meetings in 1999. Together with Bishop Norman, Elder LaVerne leads the spiritual growth and community outreach of JLAL.

Council of Elders

Pastor Kenneth and Minister Tonya Norman serve on the Elders Council, bringing over 30 years of ministry experience.

October 10, 1999
First service at Fairview Elementary School with 7 people.
2000
Growth moves ministry to 3612 Melrose Ave, Roanoke.

Church acquires edifice at 702 Ferdinand Ave SW.
