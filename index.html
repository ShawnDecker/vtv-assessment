About JLAL
A Non-Denominational Church on the Move

Jesus Is Lord ~ Assembly of Love is a non-denominational church centered on Jesus Christ and on the Holy Word of God. Under the leadership of Bishop Steven and Elder LaVerne Norman, this ministry continues to grow throughout Roanoke, Virginia — sharing God's love and increasing lives through the power of the Gospel.

📜 Our Beliefs

We are centered on Jesus Christ and on the Holy Word of God. Through the teachings of the Bible, outreach, and community, we share the good news of Jesus Christ both formally and informally — to large groups and one on one.

Our Journey
From 7 Members to a Growing Ministry

The story of Jesus Is Lord ~ Assembly of Love — birthed through prayer, grown through faith.

Summer 1999
Prayer meetings begin with Pastor Steven Norman, the late Evangelist Peggy Stuart, and Elder LaVerne Norman.
October 10, 1999
First service held at Fairview Elementary School with 7 people — 6 charter members.
2000
Church growth moves the ministry to a rented facility at 3612 Melrose Ave, Roanoke.
2006
JLAL acquires a church edifice at 702 Ferdinand Ave SW in southwest Roanoke.
March 6, 2016
Steven Norman is consecrated to the office of Bishop.
Present
Ministry continues to grow at 4142 Melrose Ave NW, reaching Roanoke and beyond through in-person worship and live broadcasts.

Meet Our Pastors

👑 Bishop Steven A. Norman

Founder and Senior Pastor. In 1999, Bishop Norman heard God's voice calling him to pastoral ministry. Consecrated to the office of Bishop on March 6, 2016. Under his leadership, JLAL has grown from 7 members to a thriving ministry impacting Roanoke and beyond.

💕 Elder LaVerne Norman
