Time Multiplier (0.1x – 2.0x) — Defensible IP

Time is applied as a mathematical multiplier to all other pillar scores. No competing framework does this. A person scoring perfectly on People, Influence, Numbers, and Knowledge but poorly on Time sees their entire score collapse. This creates urgency, drives coaching conversions, and is defensible intellectual property.

20 Cross-Pillar Impact Relationships

Each pillar directionally impacts the other four, generating 20 prescriptive recommendations. This turns a score into a roadmap. It is the engine behind coaching revenue and the reason enterprise customers pay premium.

Privacy-First Enterprise Architecture

Employees control which pillar data their employer can see. Employers receive aggregate team insights without individual scores. No competing assessment platform (DiSC, CliftonStrengths, Gallup Q12) offers this. It is the enterprise sales unlock — the reason HR departments will choose VTV over incumbents.
