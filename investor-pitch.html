Appendix F — Risk Factors

  • Market risk: Personal development is competitive. Differentiation depends on sustained privacy advantage and AI capability.
  • Execution risk: Scaling from founder-led to team-led requires hiring, process, and culture investment.
  • Enterprise sales cycle: B2B deals take 3–6 months. Revenue ramp may lag projections by 1–2 quarters.
  • Technology risk: AI landscape changes rapidly. Platform must continuously adapt to new models.
  • Regulatory risk: Data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA) require ongoing compliance investment.
  • Key person risk: Current operations are founder-dependent. Early hires must reduce this dependency.
  • Dating market risk: Highly competitive; user acquisition costs may be higher than projected.
  • Real estate risk: Appraisal index accuracy must be validated at scale before full market entry.
  • Merger integration risk: FitCarna integration requires operational alignment and shared technology adoption.
