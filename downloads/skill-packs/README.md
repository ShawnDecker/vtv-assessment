# VTV Top-10 Professionals Skill Pack Bundle

Ten focused playbooks applying the **Value to Victory 5-pillar framework** (Time · People · Influence · Numbers · Knowledge) to the daily work of ten distinct professional groups.

Each pack contains:
- **Voice rules** — how this profession should sound on the page and in the room
- **5 templates** — first-touch, follow-up, re-engagement, stage-up, 90-day check-in
- **3 AI prompts** — paste-and-go for Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any assistant
- **Weekly metrics dashboard** — what to track + decision rules when numbers slip

## What's in the bundle

1. **Real Estate Professionals** — agents, brokers, appraisers, lenders
2. **Coaches & Therapists** — life, business, counseling, therapy
3. **Christian Authors & Speakers** — book authors, conference speakers, podcast hosts
4. **Pastors & Ministry Leaders** — senior pastors, youth, missions, board
5. **Solo Consultants & Advisors** — independents, fractional execs
6. **Small Business Owners** — owner-operators, family business
7. **Event Producers & Tournament Directors** — sports, fundraising, conferences
8. **Service Professionals** — concierge, lifestyle, hospitality
9. **Content Creators & Influencers** — YouTube, TikTok, IG, podcasts, newsletters
10. **Sales Professionals** — AEs, sales managers, BDR/SDR

## How to use

1. Pick the pack that matches your profession (or the closest fit)
2. Replace `{placeholder}` text with your specifics — once, save it back
3. Drop the AI prompts into your assistant; iterate on the output
4. Set up the metrics dashboard in a spreadsheet or Notion page
5. Re-read your pack at the end of each week — most folks adjust 1-2 voice rules over their first month
