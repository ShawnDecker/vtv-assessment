## 1 · Voice rules for Event Producers & Tournament Directors

- Be specific. Vague language costs trust. Replace "we'll see" with "I'll get back to you Tuesday by 3pm."
- Lead with the decision needed, not your process. Audience doesn't care that you researched 12 options.
- One ask per message. If you list 3, you'll get zero.
- Reference the prior touchpoint. "Following up on Tuesday's call about X" beats "Just checking in."
- Numbers beat adjectives. "Saved my last client $18,400 in 60 days" beats "great results."

## 2 · Templates (5)

### T1 — First-touch outreach
> Hi {first_name}, saw {specific_thing_from_their_business}. I help {audience_segment} cut {specific_problem} by {specific_outcome}. If you'd find a 12-min walkthrough useful, here's my calendar: {calendly_link}.

### T2 — Post-meeting follow-up
> {first_name} — recap from today: 1) {decision_made} 2) {next_step_with_owner_and_date} 3) {open_question_assigned_to_them}. If I've got #2 wrong, reply by {day} and I'll adjust.

### T3 — Re-engagement (silent prospect)
> {first_name}, you went quiet after {what_happened}. Two possibilities: 1) priorities shifted, in which case zero offense and we close the loop, or 2) my last note didn't land. Which is it?

### T4 — Stage-up offer
> Quick numbers: in {timeframe}, {recent_outcome}. The next bracket where {their_business_type} sees the biggest jump is {specific_capability}. I do this as a {one-time package OR retainer} starting at ${price}. Want me to send the scope doc?

### T5 — Calendar-friendly check-in (existing client)
> {first_name}, 90-day mark check-in. Three numbers I'm tracking: {metric_1}, {metric_2}, {metric_3}. Here's where each is vs. baseline → {short_table}. One adjustment I'd make for the next 90: {specific_change}. Worth a 20-min call this week?

## 3 · AI prompts (paste into your assistant)

### P1 — "Audit my draft"
> You are a senior Event Producers & Tournament Directors advisor. Review the draft below for: vagueness, missing dates, multiple asks, weak openers, fluffy adjectives, and inconsistencies with the recipient's prior touchpoint. Rewrite with all issues fixed. Keep the original intent. Output only the rewritten draft. Draft: {paste}

### P2 — "Plan my week"
> I'm a Event Producers & Tournament Directors. Here are my 3 active deals/clients and their state: {paste}. Build a 5-day plan that maximizes weakest-pillar progress (Time, People, Influence, Numbers, or Knowledge). For each day, give 1 high-impact action ≤ 90 min, the script for any outreach, and the one number that proves it worked.

### P3 — "Diagnose this stuck deal"
> I have a Event Producers & Tournament Directors client/deal stuck for {timeframe}. Here's the history: {paste}. Tell me: most likely root cause (one sentence), the specific message I should send next, what to do if they respond vs. don't respond within 72 hours.
