Introduction

Value to Victory ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Shawn E. Decker, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Value Engine Assessment at assessment.valuetovictory.com and our related services at valuetovictory.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Your name and email address, provided when you take the assessment or sign up for our services.
  • Assessment data: Your answers to assessment questions, pillar scores, Master Value Score, score range classification, and assessment mode/depth selections.
  • Cognitive profiling data: Inferred demographic and behavioral insights derived from your assessment responses, including weakest pillar identification and cross-pillar impact analysis.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our site, collected via Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID: G-MJ1SXZJ399), including pages visited, session duration, and device/browser information.
  • Payment information: If you purchase coaching, VictoryPath, or other products, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate your personalized Value Engine assessment results and Master Value Score.
  • Send you your assessment report via email.
  • Provide personalized recommendations, prescriptions, and coaching insights.
  • Improve our assessment questions and scoring algorithms.
  • Communicate with you about our products and services (you may opt out at any time).
  • Analyze site usage to improve the user experience.
  • Process payments for premium services via Stripe.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure Neon Postgres database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal data storage in your browser:

  • localStorage: We store your email address locally to improve your experience on return visits (e.g., pre-loading personalized questions). This is not a cookie and is not sent to third parties.
  • Google Analytics 4: GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish users and sessions. These cookies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not use third-party tracking cookies beyond Google Analytics.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): For website analytics and usage tracking.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing.
  • Gmail: For sending personalized assessment report emails.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing its use of your data.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the rights listed above plus:

  • The right to restrict processing of your personal data.
  • The right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (provided when you submit the assessment) and our legitimate interest in providing and improving our services.
