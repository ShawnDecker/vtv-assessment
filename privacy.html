Aligned Hearts (Dating Service)

If you use VTV Aligned Hearts at /aligned-hearts, the following additional terms apply:

Information We Collect

  • Profile data: Display name, gender, date of birth, height, body type, faith and denomination, bio, recreation and general interests, age preferences.
  • Photos: You upload up to 6 photos. Photos are stored on our servers (currently base64-encoded in our database; we are migrating to dedicated image storage).
  • Geolocation: If you grant browser permission, we collect approximate latitude/longitude to compute distance and search radius. You can disable map visibility and distance display in settings at any time.
  • Religious affiliation: Faith and denomination are sensitive/special-category data under GDPR Art. 9 and similar laws. By providing this information you give explicit consent for us to process and display it to other users for matching purposes.
  • Activity data: Swipes (left/right), matches, messages, blocks, and reports.
  • Assessment data: Your P.I.N.K. assessment scores power compatibility matching. Other users see your 5 pillar scores on your card; they do not see sub-category breakdowns or individual answers.

How We Use It

To provide matching, compatibility scoring, messaging between matches, distance filtering, safety enforcement (blocks, reports, abuse detection), and notification emails when you receive a like, match, or message.

Sharing

Your profile (name, age, photos once interest-matched, faith, bio, P.I.N.K. pillar totals, approximate location) is visible to other Aligned Hearts members of your specified gender and age range within your search radius. We do not sell dating profile data. We do not share dating data with VTV's enterprise or coaching products without your explicit opt-in.

Retention & Deletion

You can deactivate your dating profile at any time in settings. To permanently delete your dating profile and all associated data (photos, matches, messages), email privacy@valuetovictory.com with the subject "Aligned Hearts deletion request." We will process within 30 days. Note: messages you sent to others may persist in their inboxes per their own retention.

Safety

Use the in-app block and report tools for any user violating community standards. We review reports within 72 hours. We may suspend or remove accounts for harassment, impersonation, illegal content, or repeated policy violations.

Age Requirement

Aligned Hearts is for users 18 and over. We verify date of birth at sign-up. False age representation results in account termination.
