Frequently Asked Questions
How does billing work? ▼
All plans are billed monthly through Stripe. Your subscription renews automatically each month. You can cancel at any time from your account settings and retain access through the end of your billing period.
How do I share access with my partner? ▼
On the Value Builder or Victory VIP plan, both partners create their own profiles and link accounts using each other's email address. Once linked, you'll see shared reports and can start the Couple Challenge together.
Can I add my teen to my account? ▼
Yes. Any paying parent can add children aged 13–17 for free as dependents under their account. Teens will only see age-appropriate, teen-safe tools and assessments. Children under 13 cannot be added.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Absolutely. You can cancel anytime. You'll continue to have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing cycle. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
