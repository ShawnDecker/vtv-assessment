### Ad #1 · Pre-List Valuation (target: Carolyn persona)

**Audience:**
- Geo: Chatham, Bryan, Effingham counties
- Age: 50-72
- Demographics: Homeowners, household income $150k+
- Behaviors: Active on Facebook, engaged with home/lifestyle content

**Copy variants:**

**Hook A:** "I'll show you what the comparables show before I'll tell you my listing strategy."
**Hook B:** "After 21 years of appraising Savannah homes, here's how I think about pricing differently."
**Hook C:** "What an appraiser-trained perspective sees in your Savannah home that a CMA doesn't."

**Body (consistent across hooks):**
"I'm a Realtor and a GA Certified Residential Appraiser — one of the only ones in Savannah with both. When you're thinking about selling, the right starting point isn't 'who will list me at the highest number.' It's 'who will walk me through what the comparables show.' Let's have a 30-minute conversation. No pressure, no pitch — a strategic pre-list comparable analysis grounded in 21 years of valuing the Lowcountry."

**Disclaimer (in body or footer):** *Pre-list comparable analysis is a strategic pricing review — not a USPAP appraisal.*

**CTA:** "Book Your Pre-List Analysis"
**Destination:** ManyChat → keyword `SELL` → Microsoft Bookings link

**Image direction:** Savannah homeowner couple (60s, mixed-demographic) on a porch with morning coffee. Live oak with Spanish moss in soft background. Golden hour. Subtle pearl-mark overlay top-right.

### Ad #2 · Relocation Discovery (target: Eleanor persona)

**Audience:**
- Geo: NYC, NJ, CT, DC metro, Northern VA, San Francisco Bay, LA, Boston
- Age: 42-65

Tell me where you're coming from and what you're hoping for."

**CTA:** "Plan Your Move"
**Destination:** ManyChat → keyword `MOVE` → Microsoft Bookings link

**Image direction:** Multi-generational, multi-ethnic family walking Forsyth Park, fountain in soft focus. Warm afternoon light. Subtle "From [origin] to Savannah" overlay if creative supports.

**Decision thresholds (apply Friday):**
- If **cost per ManyChat capture > $25** after $50 ad spend → kill the worst-performing hook variant
- If **CTR < 0.8%** → kill the worst-performing creative
- If **ManyChat → BoldTrail conversion < 30%** → diagnose: is the keyword reply funnel broken?

## What kills Lane 1 (escalation triggers)

- Meta ad account flagged or restricted → escalate immediately to Meta Business Support
- Microsoft Bookings page unavailable for 24+ hours → emergency fix
- BoldTrail webhook silent for 24+ hours → emergency Make scenario investigation
- Single booked consult complaining about pre-call mismatch → review the ManyChat reply, the welcome email, and the prep email; one of them is creating a wrong expectation
