### Worth knowing about Savannah

- **Hurricane season** runs June 1 – November 30. Set up your evacuation plan early. Mid-state Georgia (Macon area) is the standard inland destination.
- **Pollen season** is real, especially March-April. Your car will turn yellow. Don't take it personally.
- **Spanish moss** is harmless to people and trees but will harbor mosquitoes in summer. Don't pull it down — it's protected character.
- **Tide changes** are dramatic — 6-8 feet of swing twice a day. Plan dock activities accordingly.
- **City permits** for any major exterior work in historic districts can take time. Plan ahead.

## What to expect from me over the next year

### Day 14 — review request
You'll get a short email with one-click links to leave a Google / Zillow / Facebook review. If everything went smoothly, that's the easiest favor you can do for me. If something didn't go smoothly, please tell me first — I'd rather hear directly than read it elsewhere.
