## The three lanes at a glance

### Lane 1 — Sales (direct response)
Goal: book qualified consults this month.
Channels: Meta Ads → ManyChat → BoldTrail. Microsoft Bookings as the close.
Success metric: 8–15 booked Pre-List or Buyer Discovery calls in 30 days.

### Lane 2 — Marketing (awareness + content)
Goal: build the audience that compounds for the next 12 months.
Channels: Instagram (Reels primary), YouTube (long-form + Shorts via Repurpose.io), Blog (WordPress SEO), TikTok (cross-post), Facebook (community).
Success metric: 25% lift in IG followers, 100+ new newsletter subscribers, 3–5 blog posts ranking in top 20 for target keywords.

### Lane 3 — Relational (past client + community + referral)
Goal: reactivate the SOI and turn happy clients into a referral engine.
Channels: Outlook personal touches, Trello reminders, in-person events, handwritten notes.
Success metric: 2–4 referrals from past clients, 10+ Google/Zillow reviews collected, 1 small client appreciation event.

## The six agents

Each agent is a Claude / ChatGPT prompt that produces brand-aligned output. They're not chatbots — they're prompt files you paste in when you need a specific job done.

| Agent | Job |
|-------|-----|
| Voice Keeper | Audits any draft against Nikki's brand voice and rewrites if off-tone |
| Post Writer | Generates IG/FB/blog/email drafts in her voice, with hook + CTA |
| Follow-Up Composer | Writes stage-appropriate follow-up emails for BoldTrail flows |
| Listing Describer | Writes MLS + IG + blog descriptions for a new listing |
| SEO Briefer | Generates SEO briefs (target keyword, intent, outline, internal links) |
| Metrics Reviewer | Reads weekly Metricool + BoldTrail data and surfaces what to publish more of |

Each prompt is in `04_agents/` and ready to copy-paste.

## What this system deliberately rejects

- **Generic real estate marketing advice** — every recommendation is tied to her actual stack (BoldTrail, ManyChat, Metricool, Coffee & Contracts, Repurpose.io, PropStream, SkySlope, WordPress + iHomefinder)
- **30 different content templates** — we give 12 templates that work, not 30 that don't
- **Vanity metrics** — followers and likes are tracked but not optimized for. Booked consults and closed deals are the scoreboard.
- **"Post 3x daily everywhere"** — channel allocation matches her actual capacity (her + Muhammed) and respects the boutique brand
