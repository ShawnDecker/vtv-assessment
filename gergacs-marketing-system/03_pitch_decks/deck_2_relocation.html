What works

Three things every relocator wishes they'd known.

Light changes everything.

The neighborhood you love on Saturday morning may be unbearable on Tuesday afternoon. Spend a weekend, a weekday, and an evening before you commit.

Insurance isn't optional.

Hurricane, flood, wind — the math is real. Budget for it before you fall in love with a Tybee 1st-row home.

The marsh has seasons.

Tides, mosquitoes, marsh-grass color, sunset angle — they all shift. The "Sunday cocktail dock" looks different in February than in June.

My job is to show you the seasonal truth before you sign anything.

The neighborhoods
04 / 10

Savannah by lifestyle, not by listing site.

  • Ardsley Park — historic charm, walkable, mature live oaks. Best for: families and lifestyle professionals who want neighborhood feel.
  • Skidaway Island · The Landings — gated, golf, marsh views. Best for: empty-nesters, lifestyle retirees.
  • Tybee Island — beach town, real community. Best for: lifestyle-first buyers and second-home converters.
  • Pooler — newer construction, growing fast, suburban convenience. Best for: families, military relocations to Hunter AAF.
  • Richmond Hill (Bryan County) — quieter, lower taxes, marsh-side living. Best for: right-sizers and quieter retirees.
  • Habersham — coastal Carolina overflow, rural luxury. Best for: those willing to drive 25 minutes for what they want.
  • Historic District — squares, cobblestone, ironwork. Best for: those who'll trade parking for proximity.

Each neighborhood comes with its own honest trade-offs. Full breakdown delivered in the discovery call.
