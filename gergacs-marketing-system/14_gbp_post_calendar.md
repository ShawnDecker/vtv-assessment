# Google Business Profile · 12-Week Post Calendar

Once both Google Business Profiles are claimed (Gergacs Realty + Gergacs Appraisals), use this calendar to drive consistent weekly posts. Each post takes ~5 minutes to publish.

## Why this matters

GBP posts factor into local search ranking. A profile updated weekly outranks an identical profile updated monthly. Most Savannah agents post 0-2 GBP posts per quarter. Consistent weekly posting is compounding free local SEO.

## Posting rules

- **Length:** 100-300 words per post
- **Image:** every post needs ONE photo (1200×900 minimum, Savannah-specific per Image Library guidelines)
- **CTA:** every post has ONE button — Book / Call / Learn More / Sign Up
- **Link:** post should link to a specific page (not just home page) when possible
- **Timing:** Tuesday or Wednesday morning, ~10 AM ET
- **USPAP discipline:** any post mentioning value or pricing routes through `gergacs-uspap-checker` first

## Why posting matters more than most agents realize

Google's local search ranking is driven by:

1. **Relevance** — does the profile match what searchers are looking for? Posts reinforce relevance.
2. **Distance** — proximity to searcher (out of your control)
3. **Prominence** — how active and complete is the profile? Posts directly drive prominence score.

A profile with 52 weekly posts in a year vs a profile with 3 posts a quarter — the first will outrank the second on any "Realtor near me" search, all else equal.

This is the cheapest local SEO move in real estate. Most agents skip it because they're chasing paid ads instead.
