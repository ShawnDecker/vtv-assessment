## Lane 3 conversion math

| Stage | Conversion rate | Volume target (30 days) |
|-------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Touches sent → soft thread restored | 30-50% | 6-10 of 20 |
| Soft thread restored → referral signal | 10-20% | 1-3 |
| Referral signal → warm intro | 50-80% | 1-2 |
| Warm intro → booked consult | 60-80% | 1-2 |
| Booked consult → closed deal | 40-60% (3-9 month tail) | 1+ in 90 days |
| Review request sent → review left | 30-50% | 6-10 of past 90-day closes |

## ROI math

| Channel | Approximate cost per closed deal |
|---------|----------------------------------|
| Lane 1 (paid Meta ads) | $150-300 in ad spend per close (ignoring time) |
| Lane 2 (organic content) | $0 cash but 12 hours/week of human time × X weeks |
| **Lane 3 (relational)** | **$0-50 in event cost; ~ 30 minutes per touch × successful threads** |

Lane 3 has the highest **margin per closed deal** because the acquisition cost is essentially zero. Closing one referred client per quarter from this lane outperforms most agents' entire Lane 1 spend.

## Anti-pattern flags

- ❌ Treating Lane 3 as a "newsletter to past clients" — never templated-feeling
- ❌ Adding asks to every personal touch — the touch IS the deliverable
- ❌ Forgetting specific details about a client (use BoldTrail notes; refer before sending)
- ❌ Skipping a quarter when Nikki is busy — this lane only works with rhythm
- ❌ Reviewing for review's sake — review request must come AFTER a moment of obvious value, not on a fixed schedule with no context
- ❌ Featuring a past client publicly without explicit written permission (blog, IG, newsletter)

## Lane 3 → Other lanes

| Output | Feeds into |
|--------|-----------|
| Reviews collected | Strengthens GBP, blog, ad creative trust signals (Lane 1, Lane 2) |
| Event content (with permission) | Reels, blog posts, "Coastal Coffee" video series (Lane 2) |
| Referrals received | Tagged in BoldTrail, run through Lane 1 follow-up flows but with `referral` priority (3-5x close rate) |
| Past-client testimonials | Pitch deck assets, ad creative social proof, LinkedIn posts (Lane 2 + business development) |
