Before You Begin

This assessment explores how you and your partner connect across five dimensions of intimacy. It is designed to be thoughtful, respectful, and growth-focused — not clinical or explicit.

There are no right or wrong answers. The goal is honest self-reflection that opens the door to better communication and deeper connection.
