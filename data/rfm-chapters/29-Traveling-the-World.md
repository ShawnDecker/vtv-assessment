A Lesson Learned
I know there are certain people in your life that you want more 
from. Those things that happen, they may not be right. But 
people have free will; they go through things, sometimes hor-rible things. But no matter what the case is, you have to learn to stay afloat. The boat does not sink because of the water out-
side the ship, the ship only sinks when the water gets inside.
