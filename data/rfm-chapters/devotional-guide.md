# Running From Miracles
## 60-Day Devotional Guide

*Based on the book by Shawn E. Decker*

---

### Day 1: Introduction

**Theme:** poverty

**Scripture:** Philippians 4:19
> "And my God will meet all your needs according to the riches of his glory in Christ Jesus."

**Reflection:**
In this chapter, Shawn shares about what it means to have nothing and still find everything in God's provision. Sometimes life throws us into situations we never asked for. But God is in the details — even the ones that hurt.

**Prayer:**
Lord, thank You for Shawn's testimony about poverty. Help me to see Your hand in my own poverty. Give me eyes to see the miracles I've been running from. In Jesus' name, Amen.

**Today's Step:**
If you're in a season of lack, write down three things you DO have. Gratitude shifts perspective.
