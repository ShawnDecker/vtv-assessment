A Lesson Learned
DON’T MESS WITH A PRAYING MOM! You’re asking for 
trouble, especially as persistent and patient as my mom is. 
That woman is truly tenacious. I wonder if she would have given up if I would have come back home?  I may never know, but I do know, if you have someone that you have a strained or 
separated relationship with, don’t ever, ever give up on them!  
God can do great things because of your persistence.
