A Lesson Learned
Some of the things that you will pursue the most will cause 
you the most pain. Fame, popularity, sex, attention, relation-
ships, and money are all fleeting. This is something many of celebrities and others have learned, many times just before killing themselves. Without people that love you and someone 
to share your life with, what is the real purpose?  Many things 
that you pursue are the reasons you are causing the trouble in your life.
Prayer
Give me wisdom to see what is really important and what is not, and help me to peruse things that are. Let me see the difference between what is good and what is God, what 
is fun and what is fake, what is my future and what will be
my failure.
