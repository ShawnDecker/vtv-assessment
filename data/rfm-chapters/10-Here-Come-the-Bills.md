A Lesson Learned
I know now that a lot of these things were not God’s will, but 
due to not knowing how to hear his voice, what his promises 
are, and different ways to come out of these problems, we spent much longer in some of these situations than we had to. Even still, I have learned that if you will be diligent and faith-
ful, God will always help you to come out of every situation, no 
matter how impossible it may seem.
