A Lesson Learned
Most marriages never have a chance because they never spend 
any time to ask questions. They never get to know each other, 
their problems, sins, attitudes, etc., and the marriage really never has a chance. But even when you do know these things, it does not prevent the marriage from having problems. The 
only thing that will truly help the marriage to be successful is 
selflessness and Christ being the center of every decision.
