A Lesson Learned
There were many of times that I wondered if Autumn was 
my true love, soul mate, one and only, and all that fun stuff. 
But what I know is that if I would have stood in that mindset, that many things in the future would have not happened. I am confident that I would have probably not written this book, 
that I would not have had my current son, and that I would be 
in a completely different place in my life. I am pretty sure that would not have been a better place than where I am today.
