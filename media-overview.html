The Story

From Appraising Houses to Appraising Lives

Shawn Decker spent years as a certified real estate appraiser — trained to determine the true value of a property by examining what's underneath the surface. One day, he realized: people need the same thing.

Most people don't know their own value. They're stuck in survival mode, underpricing themselves in relationships, careers, and life. Shawn built the Value Engine — a proprietary AI-powered assessment platform — to change that.

"I went from appraising million-dollar homes to appraising the priceless value inside people. The framework is the same — you look at the foundation, the structure, and the potential."
— Shawn E. Decker, Founder
The Framework

P.I.N.K. — Five Pillars of Personal Value

The assessment measures 50 dimensions across 5 life pillars, producing a Master Score from 0-250 that reveals exactly where someone is — and what to do next.

T
Time
How you invest your hours
P
People
Who you surround yourself with
I
Influence
The impact you have on others
N
Numbers
Your financial awareness
K
Knowledge
How you learn and apply

Each pillar has 10 sub-categories, scored 1–5. The result isn't just a number — it's a personalized prescription with a 30-day action plan, coaching emails, and accountability check-ins powered by AI.
